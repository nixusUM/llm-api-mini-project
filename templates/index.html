How to Verify

1) Turn OFF compression and send 3-4 messages. Save token values.
2) Turn ON compression, keep_last_n=8, summarize_every_n=10, continue dialog.
3) Compare history full/effective tokens and savings in metrics.
4) Continue dialog and compare response quality with and without compression.
5) Review token and estimated cost trends in `Token Growth`.
