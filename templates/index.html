How to Verify Day 10

1) Run same 10-15 message scenario in each strategy.
2) Sliding: watch important details drop after window.
3) Facts: verify key facts persist with lower effective history.
4) Branching: create checkpoint + 2 branches and diverge decisions.
5) Compare quality, stability, tokens and usability.
